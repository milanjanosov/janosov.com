The new season of Game of Thrones is almost upon us and fans are excited about what it may bring. I am probably not alone in wondering which of my favourite characters are going to meet their ends, and which will live on to the next season. So I decided to come up with a ranking for the characters based on how likely it is they will die. Game of Thrones is a complex world in which social position and true friends seem to be quite important, so I quantified each characterâ€™s social interaction patterns using the tools of network science. I then predict their fate using machine learning methods.
